YOUTH RESOURCE APP

What is the Youth Resource App?

The Youth Resource App (YRA) is a free resource for all Portland youth, funded by Portland Public Schools Office of Student Support Services and created by the Student Success and Health mental health advisory team along with the feedback of over 100 PPS students. The cover art was designed by a Grant HS graduate.

The app has information about suicide prevention; heath and wellness; housing; food; LGBTQIA+ resources; immigrant and refugee resources and more.

How are the resources chosen?

There are many amazing resources in our community not listed on this app. Resources are primarily chosen by capacity to support large numbers as well as cultural responsiveness and specific identity supports.

Is it collecting youth information?

The YRA will be able to see how many people view the app but it does not collect personal information.

What about youth privacy?

The YRA is a landing place for links to agency and resources web pages. Once the user moves on to those pages, those organizations' privacy policies apply. We recommend users review the privacy policies of the organizations that hold interest for them.

What if I see a mistake or a change that should be made?

Feel free to email feedback to youthresourceapp@pps.net
